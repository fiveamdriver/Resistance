# Demo Power Board — Requirements (Sample)

> Mock document for Phase 1 testing. Contains no proprietary data.

## Power

- The 5V rail shall supply up to 3A continuous with less than 1% ripple.
- The input voltage range shall be 7V to 24V DC.
- The regulator (U7) shall include input and output decoupling.

## Mechanical

- The board shall fit within a 50 mm x 50 mm outline.
- Mounting holes shall be 3.2 mm diameter at each corner.

## Compliance

- The design shall meet CISPR 32 Class B radiated emissions.
